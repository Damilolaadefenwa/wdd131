NATURE PARKS IN LAGOS

Discover the natural beauty and unique experiences that this Lagos parks have to offer. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a chance to connect with wildlife, these destinations provide something for everyone.

Lufasi Nature park

Escape the Lagos bustle and dive into the wild heart of Lufasi Nature Park. This sprawling green oasis on the Lekki-Epe Expressway offers an exhilarating blend of adventure and tranquility. Wander lush forest trails, discover a thriving animal sanctuary with rescued wildlife like monkeys and tortoises, and soak in the serene beauty of its lakes. Perfect for families, nature lovers, and anyone seeking a refreshing escape, Lufasi promises unforgettable encounters with nature, exciting activities like picnics and sports, and a vital connection to Lagos's precious biodiversity. Come explore, breathe deep, and experience the magic!
To learn more about their opening hours, admission fees, and available amenities, visit their official website at Lufasi Nature Park. Here, you can find detailed information about the park.

Omu Resort

Prepare for a day of non-stop excitement at Omu Resort, Lagos's ultimate family destination! This sprawling park seamlessly blends thrilling amusement rides, a fantastic water park, and a fascinating zoo with a commitment to conservation and education. Get up close with diverse wildlife, conquer exhilarating slides, challenge your friends on the go-kart track, or simply relax amidst beautiful natural surroundings. Omu Resort promises an unforgettable escape where adventure meets nature, making it the perfect spot for creating cherished memories with loved ones.
To learn more about their opening hours, admission fees, and available amenities, visit their official website at Omu Resort Lagos ..

Lekki Conservation Center

Get ready for an adventure at Lekki Conservation Centre,Lagos's crown jewel of nature! Home to Africa's longest canopy walkway, this thrilling aerial stroll offers breathtaking panoramic views of the lush forest and its vibrant inhabitants. Beyond the heights, explore serene boardwalks teeming with monkeys, exotic birds, and even crocodiles, or challenge friends to life-sized board games in the family park. Its the perfect escape to reconnect with nature,learn about vital conservation efforts,and create unforgettable memories in the heart of the city.
